To Search Menu details:
1) Navigate to the 'Database' tab from the tab bar
2) In there navigate to 'Menu'.
3) Select 'Item Type' from the drop-down list infront of its attribute.
4) Select 'Search By' from the drop-down list infront of its attribute.
5) Enter the filter for 'Search By' below it.
6) Finally click 'Search'.
7) Also, you can press 'Export to Excel file' to choose where to save the result as a spreadsheet.
