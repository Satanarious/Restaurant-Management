1) The topmost line contains the name of the restaurant which can be changed(see 1.2)
2) The Details are at the bottom:
        i) The first is the 'login date', which tells the last login date.
       ii) The second is the 'login time', which tells the last login time.
      iii) The third is the 'No. of Tables served(Today)', which is the total number of tables served that particular day.
       iv) The fourth is the 'No. of Tables served(Till Today)', which is the total number of tables served from the very beginning till that time.
        v) The fifth is the 'Today's Earning', which is the total money earned that day.
       vi) The sixth is the 'Today's' Favorite', which is the most sold food item that day.
