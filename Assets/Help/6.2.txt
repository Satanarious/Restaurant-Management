To search customer details by various filters:
1) Navigate to the 'Database' tab from the tab bar
2) In there navigate to 'Customer details'.
3) There are 6 categories for filtering.
4) Choose single or multiple category(s).
5) Enter the search details in their respective groupboxes.
6) Finally click 'Search'.
7) The Result count is displayed.
8) Click the 'Export to Excel file' and choose where to save a spreadsheet of the result
