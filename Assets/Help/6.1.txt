To login into the Database:
1) Navigate to the 'Database' tab from the tab bar
2) Enter the password next to the 'Password' attribute.
3) Click authenticate.
4) The text at the bottom will change to 'Access Granted'
5) Now all the tabs right to the permit tab are accessable.
6) To logout, click 'Unauthenticate'.
7) The text at the bottom will change to 'Access Denied'
8) Now all the tabs right to the permit tab are unaccessable.
