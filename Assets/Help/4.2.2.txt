To modify an item:
1) Navigate to the 'Modify Menu' tab from the tab bar.
2) In the 'Modify Menu' tab navigate to 'Edit Item' sub-tab.
3) In the 'Delete Item' groupbox:
       i) Select item type from the drop-down list next to 'Item Type' attribute.
      ii) Enter the unique Item Id next to the 'Item Id' attribute.
     iii) Finally click 'Search'.
      iv) This should display the required item(if exists) in the Item Detail area at the bottom of the groupbox.
4) In the 'Edit Item' groupbox:
       i) Now the Edit Item Buttons will be activated with 'Change' written on them.
      ii) Enter/Choose the value you want to edit infront of the given catagories.
     iii) To reflect the changes, Press the 'Change' button infront of the particular category.
      iv) A Successs prompt will be displayed.
