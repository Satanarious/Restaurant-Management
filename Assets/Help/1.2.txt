To change the Restaurant's name(Home page title):
1) Simply navigate to the Setting's tab from the tab bar.
2) In the Setting's tab select the second groupbox that says 'Change Restaurant Name'.
3) Now to change various possible attributes of the title:
      i) Write the Name in front of the 'Name' attribute(default 'The Restaurant').
     ii) Change the font size from the spinner next to the 'Font Size' attribute(default '41').
    iii) Change the font face from the drop down list next to 'Font Face' attribute(defaul 'Segoe UI Black').
     iv) Finally click on 'Change' on the same groupbox to reflect the changes.
      v) You can also press the 'Reset' button next to the 'Change' button to reset all attributes to the last saved presets.
