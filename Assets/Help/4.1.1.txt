To add a single new item:
1) Navigate to the 'Modify Menu' tab from the tab bar.
2) In the 'Modify Menu' tab navigate to 'Add New Item' sub-tab.
3) In this tab select the item type from the drop-down list next to 'Item Type' attribute.
4) Now enter the item Id next to'Item Id' attribute.
5) Now enter the item Name next to'Item Name' attribute.
6) Next, Enter the Half prize ,if exists, next to the 'Half Price' attribute.
7) Next, Enter the Full prize ,if exists, next to the 'Full Price' attribute.
8) Lastly, Click on the 'Add Item' button to submit the entry.
9) Also, Press the 'Reset' button to set all fields to default or blank.
